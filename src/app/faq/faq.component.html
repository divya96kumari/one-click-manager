Remote Volunteering Engagement Activities with ILP:Question Bank Activity

ILP has created digital lesson plans using MS Powerpoint for all chapters in Maths, Science and Social Science for Standard 5 to 10 that are currently being used by teachers in the 700+ schools where we work. Along with the textbook content, these PPTs contain interesting videos and visuals that make the class interesting for the students.

Objective is to supplement this lesson plan PPT with some interesting questions that the teachers can ask in the class to help students think critically about new things, and also help revise the syllabus covered. We have added questions from the textbook to the PPTs and would like your help in researching and adding more questions.

We expect our digital content to be made available on Diksha portal (https://diksha.gov.in/explore) soon, which children across the country access through the QR code given in their textbooks, so the impact that Dell volunteers create is not just for Karnataka but potentially for several states! We will share the text books, excel sheet with lesson names and numbers, folders to upload the questionnaire word file/ PPT.
